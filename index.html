About Sahara Lubricants

Nigeria's Trusted
Lubricant Manufacturer

"Engineering excellence in every drop — from our plant to your machine."

Sahara Lubricants Ltd is a Nigerian lubricant manufacturing company headquartered at Plot No. 11689, Cadastral Zone A15, Auta Baleifi, Karu LGA, Nasarawa State. We specialise in the formulation, blending, packaging, and nationwide distribution of premium-grade lubricants — including engine oils, gear oils, hydraulic fluids, greases, and industrial lubricants.

Our products are engineered to meet and exceed international quality standards, providing superior protection and performance for automotive, industrial, agricultural, marine, and construction applications across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Production Process

From raw base oils to finished, packaged product — every step is precision-controlled.

Raw Material Sourcing

Blending & Formulation

Standards & Compliance

Quality Assurance & Compliance

Our commitment to quality is not a promise — it is a documented, tested, and verified process embedded in every stage of production.

In-House Laboratory Testing

Every batch undergoes comprehensive testing — viscosity grade, flash point, pour point, total acid number, and density — before release.

Regulatory Certifications

Our products and operations comply with regulatory requirements from SON (Standards Organisation of Nigeria), DPR/NMDPRA, and relevant international bodies.

API Performance Standards

All engine oils are blended to meet API (American Petroleum Institute) classifications, ensuring compatibility and performance with modern engine technologies.

Raw Material Verification

Each incoming base oil and additive consignment is tested on arrival and cross-referenced against supplier certificates of analysis before use.

Good Manufacturing Practice

Our blending facility operates under GMP guidelines, with documented procedures, equipment calibration schedules, and traceability records for every batch.

Trained Technical Team

Our quality control personnel are trained chemists and chemical engineers who understand lubricant formulation, testing protocols, and performance standards.

Operational Standards

Our production framework is built around six non-negotiable pillars.

Standardised production processes and laboratory testing protocols

Batch traceability across every production run

International specification compliance and continuous performance evaluation

Zero-compromise health, safety, and environmental (HSE) standards

Workplace safety enforcement and hazard-prevention systems

Spill prevention and environmental impact mitigation
